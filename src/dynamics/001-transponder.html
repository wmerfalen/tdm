Transponder Signal

  • Will alert nearby NPC's/Players that you are a friendly

Example: Explicit activation

  1. Activate transponder
  2. Walk into room with hostile NPC's
  3. NPC's will not attack
  4. Player attacks an NPC within the room
  5. Transponder no longer active
  6. Transponder ability takes T to recharge
  7. Ability no longer active until fully recharged and activated again manually
  8. N% chance to catch NPC off guard
  9. If NPC caught off guard, NPC's turn to do damage is skipped
